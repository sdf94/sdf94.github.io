I am Sarah Floris, and I am interested in data

I first graduated as a chemistry major with a mathematics minor. While I was researching in the Chemistry PhD program at University of Washington, I was accepted into a data science program. This program was the first time I heard about data analysis realm, and it inspired me to pursue data science as a career. Following, I graduated from University of Washington with a Master of Science in Computational Chemistry and enrolled into Springboard, an online data science curriculum.

Experience

ILest: Ionic Liquid estimation tool

I implemented LASSO, SVR, and MLP-Regressor on 10,000 data points to estimate the desired properties of ionic liquid binary mixtures for electric battery applications Tools: scikit-learn, Pandas, RDKit, Matplotlib

Data-Driven Design of Perovskite

I carried out LASSO, and supervised neural networks on a reduced data set to identify the optimal combination of elements in the hybrid organic perovskites (crystals) Tools: scikit-learn, Pandas, RDKit, Matplotlib
